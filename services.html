Our Services

Weekly Services

Sunday Worship Service

8:00 AM & 10:30 AM
Main Sanctuary

Experience powerful worship, inspiring messages, and a warm community. Our Sunday services feature live worship music, practical Bible teaching, and opportunities for prayer.

  • Live Worship Band
  • Expository Preaching
  • Children's Ministry Available
  • Fellowship Breakfast (9:30 AM)

Wednesday Prayer Meeting

6:00 PM - 7:30 PM
Prayer Chapel

Midweek spiritual renewal through prayer, intercession, and worship. Join us as we seek God's face together for our church, community, and nation.

  • Group Prayer Sessions
  • Intercessory Prayer
  • Testimony Sharing
  • Anointing Service (Last Wednesday)

Friday Bible Study

7:00 PM - 8:30 PM
Fellowship Hall

Deepen your understanding of God's Word through interactive verse-by-verse Bible study. Perfect for spiritual growth and building strong biblical foundations.

  • Verse-by-Verse Teaching
  • Group Discussion
  • Q&A Session
  • Study Materials Provided

Children's Church

10:30 AM (Sunday)
Children's Wing

Age-appropriate teaching and activities for children ages 3-12. Safe, fun, and educational environment where kids learn about God's love.

  • Interactive Bible Lessons
  • Kids Worship Songs
  • Arts & Crafts
  • Fun Activities & Games

Youth Service

4:00 PM (Saturday)
Youth Center

Dynamic worship and relevant teaching for teenagers and young adults (ages 13-25). Addressing real-life issues from a biblical perspective.

  • Contemporary Worship
  • Youth Relevant Topics
  • Fellowship & Snacks
  • Sports & Games

Small Groups

Various Times
Throughout the Week

Connect with others in intimate small group settings across the city. Perfect for building meaningful relationships and growing together in faith.

  • Home-Based Groups
  • Workplace Groups
  • Men's & Women's Groups
  • Couples & Singles Groups
