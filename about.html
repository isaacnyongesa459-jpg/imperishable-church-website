Our Story

Imperishable Crown Bible Baptist Church was founded in 2020 during the challenging season of the COVID-19 pandemic. What started with just five committed believers meeting faithfully despite the uncertainty grew steadily as God opened doors and more people were reached with the Gospel.

Through prayer, perseverance, and God's grace, the church expanded into a warm spiritual family. Today, Imperishable Crown Bible Baptist Church joyfully welcomes 80–100 worshippers every Sunday for fellowship, worship, and the teaching of God's Word.

Our Motto

What We Believe

  • The Bible – The inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God.
  • The Trinity – One God in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • Salvation – Salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.
  • The Church – The body of Christ called to worship, fellowship, discipleship, ministry, and mission.
  • The Return of Christ – Jesus Christ will return visibly and victoriously to judge the living and the dead.
